Season Menu - Watermelon Feta Salad
It needs 1 colve garlic, minced or grated. And 1 small Fresno chile, olive oil, red wine vinegar, Kosher salt and freshly grounded black pepper, chopped fresh mint, water melon, english cucumber, scallions, and feta cheese.
Two chicken breasts
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
Black pepper, Olive oil
Sprinkle the chicken with salt and pepper and drizzle with the olive oil.
Garlic, Fresno, Olive oil, Vinegar
Add the garlic, Fresno, olive oil and vinegar to a glass jar with a lid.
Pepper, Mint
Season with salt and pepper, then add the mint.
Watermelon, Cucumber
Combine watermelon and cucumber and scallions in a large bowl. Dress with the vinaigrette and gently toss to combine. Sprinkle the crumbled feta on top. Serve chilled.
