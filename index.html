Specials

Monday : Dolma ingredients preparation method
  1. Grape leaves
  2. Ground beef or lamb
  3. Rice
  4. Onion
  5. Tomato paste
  1. Mix together ground beef or lamb, rice, onion, and tomato paste in a bowl.
  2. Wrap the mixture in grape leaves.
  3. Cook the dolma in a pot of water for 30-40 minutes until the rice is cooked and the meat is tender.
Tuesday : Fried shrimps in sauce ingredients preparation method
  1. Shrimp
  2. Tomatoes
  3. Olive oil
  4. Black pepper
  5. Onion
  6. Garlic cloves
  1. Mix flour, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
  2. Coat shrimp in the flour mixture.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook shrimp until golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove shrimp from skillet and set aside.
  4. Grill shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  5. In the same skillet, cook onion and garlic until softened, about 2-3 minutes. Add diced tomatoes, white wine, and bring to a simmer. Cook for 5-7 minutes or until sauce has thickened slightly.
  6. Return shrimp to skillet and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes or until heated through.
Wednesday : Pasta with Shrimp ingredients preparation method
  1. Pasta
  2. Garlic
  3. Red pepper flakes
  4. Olive oil
  5. Butter
  1. Heat oil and butter in skillet.
  2. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, cook for 1 min.
  3. Add shrimp, cook for 2-3 mins per side.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Drain pasta and add to skillet with shrimp.
Thursday : Pita Bread ingredients preparation method
  1. Pita Bread
  2. Tomato
  3. Cooked chicken or lamb, sliced
  4. Onion
  5. Cucumber
  1. Stuff each pita pocket with sliced chicken or lamb, cucumber, red onion, tomato, feta cheese, Kalamata olives, and parsley.
  2. Place the pita pockets on a baking sheet and bake in the oven for about 10 minutes
  3. Or until the pita bread is slightly crispy and the filling is warm
Friday : Seafood Paella ingredients preparation method
  1. Rice
  2. Prawns
  3. Shrimp
  4. Clams
  5. Tomato
  6. Onion
  1. Pour in the stock then reduce the heat, partially cover and allow to simmer for 7 minute or until most of the liquid has been absorbed and the rice is almost cooked.
  2. Add the squid, calamari and shrimp, tucking it into the rice.
  3. Try not to disturb the bottom of the rice which should be forming a nice crust as it cooks.
  4. Cover again then cook for another 5-7 minutes until the seafood and rice are fully cooked.
Saturday : Pork Meat Ssam ingredients preparation method
  1. Pork Meat
  2. Salad Greens
  3. Dijon,wholegrain mustard
  4. Ssam
  1. Take your pork slices and cover with 1 teaspoon of olive oil and liberally apply salt to both sides.
  2. Cook in the oven until golden brown and crispy. Approximately 20-30 minutes.
  3. Make your vinaigrette by adding mustards, white wine vinegar, and olive oil into a small bowl and whisk or mix with a fork until thickened and combined well.
  4. Remove your crispy cooked pork belly from the oven and allow to cool, then slice into bite size pieces.
  5. Toss your salad greens with the vinaigrette and top with sliced pork belly, candied nuts, blue vein cheese, and pear.
Sunday : Grilled Chuck Roast ingredients preparation method
  1. Chuck Steak
  2. Dijon mustard
  3. Garlic
  4. Beef bouillon
  5. Fresh parsley
  1. Whisk the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, shallot, garlic, parsley, Dijon, sugar, beef bouillon, salt, pepper, and meat tenderizer in a bowl.
  2. Score the chuck roast with a sharp knife making 6 or 7 large cuts all over one side of the meat.
  3. Add the marinade to a plastic bag and then add the steak.
  4. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the steak. Place it on a plate to catch any drips or leaks and refrigerate for at least 6 hours, ideally overnight.
  5. Remove the roast from the grill and allow to rest loosely covered with foil, about 10 minutes.
